    Privacy PolicyforAgapeCoin4all Ltd.

    The type of personal information we collect

    We currently collect and process the following information:

    Personal information you provide by filling in forms on our website www.agapecoin4all.com ("Website"), including but not limited to the registration form and account application form.

    Your correspondence with us.

    Details of all transactions you carry out through our website and any related trading or investment platforms as well as all orders fulfilled.

    Your linked trading history.

    Information about your computer, including, where available, your IP address, operating system, and browser type.

    Details of your visits to our website, including but not limited to traffic data, location data, weblogs and other communication data.

    How we obtain personal information and why we have it

    Most of the personal information we process is provided to us directly by you for one of the following reasons:

    To use our website

    To apply for an account with us and subscribe to our services

    To answer a survey

    To report a problem with our website or services.

    We also receive personal information indirectly, from the following sources in the following scenarios:

    Own and third-party cookies that we store in your browser or on your computer's hard drive. Unless you have adjusted your browser settings to reject cookies, our system will issue cookies as soon as you visit our site.

    We use the information you have provided:

    To carry out our obligations derived from any contract between you and us.

    To provide you with information, products or services that you request from us or which, based on your web browsing behavior and email subscription preferences, we believe may be of interest to you, where you have consented to be contacted for such purposes.

    To notify you about changes to our service based on your email subscription preferences

    To comply with our regulatory obligations

    To meet our customer due diligence requirements

    To ensure that content from our website is presented in the most transparent and effective manner to our users.

    For research purposes

    We may disclose your personal information to any member of our group, which means our subsidiaries, our ultimate holding company and its subsidiaries, as defined in section 1159 of the UK Companies Act 2006.

    In the event that we sell or buy any business or assets, we may disclose your personal data to the prospective seller or buyer of such business or assets. If AgapeCoin4all Ltd or substantially all of its assets are acquired by a third party, personal data it holds about its customers will be one of the transferred assets.

    We may disclose your personal information to comply with any legal obligation, or to enforce or apply our Terms and Conditions and other agreements; or to protect the rights, property, or safety of AgapeCoin4all Ltd, our customers, and partners. This includes exchanging information with other companies and organizations for the purposes of fraud protection and credit risk reduction.

    We may also share some of your personal information with the following types of third parties:

    Web service hosting providers

    Trading Platform Hosting Providers

    Advertising Provider Centers

    Customer relationship management software providers

    "Get to know your clients" software vendors

    Banks and payment gateways

    Leading Brokerage, Clearing and Custody Service Providers

    Regulatory information technology providers

    Auditors

    Regulators and other government and intergovernmental agencies

    According to the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the legal bases on which we rely to process this information are:

    (a) Your consent. You can withdraw your consent at any time. You can do this autonomously for emails by updating your email subscription preferences or by contacting us through the email contacts provided.

    (b) We have a contractual obligation.

    (c) We have a legal obligation.

    (d) We have a legitimate interest.

    How we store your personal information

    All information you provide to us is stored on secure servers within the European Economic Area, or with providers that comply with the US and EU Safe Harbor framework as established by the US Department of Commerce.

    All payment transactions will be encrypted using SSL technology. Where we have provided you with (or where you have chosen) a password which enables you to access certain parts of our website, you are responsible for keeping this password confidential. We ask that you do not share your password with anyone.

    Unfortunately, the transmission of information over the Internet is not completely secure. Although we will do our best to protect your personal data, as far as possible for our company we want to guarantee the security of your data transmitted to our website; any transmission is at your own risk. Once we have received your information, we will use strict procedures and security features to prevent unauthorized access.

    We keep all types of personal information mentioned above for at least 5 years from the date we stop doing business with you.

    Your data protection rights

    Under data protection law, you have rights that include:

    Your right of access: You have the right to request copies of your personal information from us.

    Your right to rectification: You have the right to ask us to rectify personal information that you believe to be inaccurate or incorrect. You also have the right to ask us to complete information you think is incomplete.

    Your right to erasure: You have the right to ask us to erase your personal information in certain circumstances.

    Your right to restriction of processing: You have the right to ask us to restrict the processing of your personal information in certain circumstances.

    Your right to object to processing: You have the right to object to the processing of your personal information in certain circumstances.

    Your right to data portability: You have the right to request that we transfer the personal information you provided to us to another organization, or to you, in certain circumstances.

    You are not required to pay any fees for exercising your rights. If you make a request, we have a month to respond to you.

    Contact us through the email address provided on the website if you wish to make a request.

    • How it Works

    The below steps explain how the process works

    Register Account

    After registering on the website you will receive a registration email link

    Verify Email

    You will need to verify the email address provided for security purposes

    Verify KYC

    You will need to pass our KYC checks before being able to use the platform

    Deposit Money

    Once KYC is complete, users can deposit funds up to a maximum of $5,000 via bank transfer or Paypal. Deposits above certain values will be subject to further customer due diligence checks.

    Choose a plan

    Users can select from our range of plans according to their desired investment level.

    Transfer Money

    Users can transfer funds from their wallet to another verified user of the platform (subject to our transfer volume and frequency limits).

    Refer to Friends

    Use your referral link to receive a bonus if they sign up to any plan.

    Withdraw and Enjoy

    Withdraw funds from your wallet at any time and receive in a maximum of 2-3 business days.
